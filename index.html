Exploring how AI can become a useful instrument for scientific discovery.

I study computational approaches to biomedical discovery, with particular interests in molecular docking, de novo drug design, and medical AI.

View research Download CV

I am an undergraduate student at Seoul National University working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and biomedical science. My current interests center on computational methods that can make scientific discovery more reliable, efficient, and physically informed.

Since January 2025, I have conducted research at AIBL on molecular docking models and de novo drug design. In summer 2026, I joined the Medical Vision Lab at Seoul National University Hospital to broaden my experience in medical artificial intelligence. Ongoing research is described at a deliberately high level.
